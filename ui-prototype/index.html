Hidden Veg Pasta
A family favourite — kids taste pasta, parents know better 😉
⏱ 18 min
👨‍👩‍👧 Serves 4
⭐⭐⭐ Kid-friendly
Ingredients — from your fridge
Pasta (any shape) 300g ✓ In fridge
Spinach 1 handful ✓ In fridge
Canned tomatoes 1 tin ✓ In fridge
Carrots 2 medium ✓ In fridge
Garlic 2 cloves ⚠ Buy
Steps
1
Boil pasta according to packet. While it cooks, finely chop carrots and spinach.
2
Blend chopped veg with the canned tomatoes until smooth. Season with a pinch of salt.
3
Warm the sauce in a pan for 5 minutes. Drain pasta and stir through.
4
Serve with grated cheese on top.
💡
Kid-friendly tip: Blend the sauce completely smooth — kids won't see the spinach or carrots. Serve Mia's portion before adding any chilli or strong herbs.
